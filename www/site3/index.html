A third site, a third identity

This page lets you demonstrate another version of the same server, with a darker and more modern design while keeping the interface simple.

It is useful for showing that your webserv can respond differently depending on the requested site, instead of serving the same content everywhere.

Different host

Each site can have its own name, files, and configuration.

Different style

You can clearly show that the user is switching to another site, even on the same server.

The buttons below let you move quickly between sites during your demo.
